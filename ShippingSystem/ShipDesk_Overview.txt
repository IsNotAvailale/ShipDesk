====================================================
  ShipDesk — Complete System Overview
====================================================

WHAT IS SHIPDESK?
------------------------------------------------------
ShipDesk is a shipping management system that runs entirely
on your office computer and opens in your web browser.

It does three main things:
  1. Reads your Gmail inbox and pulls out client shipping info
  2. Keeps a database of all your clients and their addresses
  3. Creates UPS shipping labels with one click

Nothing is stored online. All data stays on your computer.
No monthly fees. No subscriptions.


====================================================
  HOW TO START SHIPDESK
====================================================

Double-click: C:\ShippingSystem\start.bat

A black window will open (do not close it — this keeps
ShipDesk running). Your browser will open automatically.

To stop ShipDesk, close the black window.


====================================================
  LOGGING IN
====================================================

When ShipDesk opens, you will see a login screen.

There are two types of accounts:

  EMPLOYEE — Can view clients, add clients, view
             pending shipments, and check emails manually.

  ADMIN    — Can do everything an employee can, PLUS:
             process and send shipments, update UPS
             settings, manage user accounts, and view logs.

The default admin account is:
  Username: admin
  Password: admin123
  (Change this immediately in Settings!)


====================================================
  PAGE BY PAGE GUIDE
====================================================

------------------------------------------------------
1. DASHBOARD (Home Screen)
------------------------------------------------------
The first page you see after logging in.

Shows you at a glance:
  - Total number of clients in the system
  - How many shipments are waiting to be processed
  - How many shipments were sent today

Also shows a preview list of pending shipments.

Button: "Check Emails Now"
  Manually tells ShipDesk to go read your Gmail inbox
  right now and look for new shipping requests.
  (ShipDesk also does this automatically every 30 minutes.)


------------------------------------------------------
2. CLIENTS (Client Database)
------------------------------------------------------
A searchable list of every client the company has ever
shipped to.

What you can do here:
  - Search by name, company, city, or email
  - Add a new client manually using the "Add Client" button
  - Edit an existing client's information
  - Click the truck icon to add a client to the shipment queue

Each client record stores:
  Name, Company, Street Address, City, State, ZIP,
  Email, Phone Number, and the date they were added.


------------------------------------------------------
3. SHIPMENTS (Shipment Queue)
------------------------------------------------------
Shows all shipments that are pending — ready to be
processed and sent to UPS.

EMPLOYEE view:
  Can see the list but cannot process shipments.
  A message will tell them to contact an admin.

ADMIN view:
  Sees the full list plus a button:
  "Confirm & Process All (X)"

  When the admin clicks that button:
    - A confirmation message appears to double-check
    - ShipDesk contacts UPS for every pending shipment
    - UPS labels are generated as PDF files
    - Labels are saved automatically to C:\ShippingSystem\labels\
    - Tracking numbers are saved to the database
    - Shipments move from "Pending" to "Completed"

  YOU WILL BE CHARGED BY UPS when you click this button.
  Always review the list carefully before confirming.

History button:
  Shows all past shipments (completed and pending),
  their tracking numbers, and a download button for
  each PDF label.


------------------------------------------------------
4. SETTINGS (Admin Only)
------------------------------------------------------
Only admins can see this page.

Divided into three sections:

  UPS API CREDENTIALS
    Where you enter your UPS account information:
    - UPS Client ID (from UPS Developer Portal)
    - UPS Client Secret (from UPS Developer Portal)
    - UPS Account Number
    - Your company's name and shipping address
      (this appears as the "From" address on every label)

    Click "Save UPS Settings" to save. Credentials are
    stored encrypted on your computer — never sent anywhere
    except directly to UPS when processing shipments.

  GMAIL SETUP
    Instructions for connecting your Gmail account.
    You place a file called gmail_credentials.json in the
    credentials folder, then authorize access the first time
    you click "Check Emails Now."

  USER ACCOUNTS
    - See all current users and their roles
    - Add new employees or admins
    - Change anyone's password
    - Delete users who no longer work there


------------------------------------------------------
5. LOGS (Admin Only)
------------------------------------------------------
A full history of everything that has happened in ShipDesk:

  - Every login and logout
  - Every client added (manually or by email agent)
  - Every shipment processed or failed
  - Every email flagged for review
  - Every settings change

You can search the logs by keyword (name, action, date, etc.)

Color coding:
  Green  = Success (client added, shipment processed)
  Yellow = Needs attention (email flagged for review)
  Red    = Something failed


====================================================
  THE EMAIL AGENT
====================================================

ShipDesk automatically reads your Gmail inbox every
30 minutes looking for emails that contain shipping info.

HOW IT WORKS:
  1. Finds unread emails in your inbox
  2. Reads the email and tries to extract:
       Name, Address, City, State, ZIP, Email, Phone
  3. If it finds a complete address → adds the client
     to the database automatically
  4. If information is missing or unclear → flags the
     email in the Logs for an admin to review manually
  5. Marks the email as "read" — never deletes anything

The email can be written in any format — freeform,
bullet points, labeled fields, doesn't matter.
ShipDesk is smart enough to find the address regardless.

MANUAL TRIGGER:
  Click "Check Emails Now" on the Dashboard to run the
  email agent immediately without waiting 30 minutes.

FLAGGED EMAILS:
  Go to Logs and search "Flagged" to see any emails where
  ShipDesk wasn't sure about the address. You can then
  add those clients manually using the Add Client form.


====================================================
  UPS LABEL DETAILS
====================================================

Every label ShipDesk generates uses these settings:

  Service:      UPS Next Day Air
  Package Type: My Packaging (your own box)
  Dimensions:   14 inches long
                10 inches wide
                 1 inch tall
  Weight:       1 pound

Labels are saved as PDF files here:
  C:\ShippingSystem\labels\

Each file is named:
  YYYY-MM-DD_ClientName_TrackingNumber.pdf

You can also download any label from the Shipment
History page by clicking the "PDF" button.


====================================================
  SECURITY
====================================================

  - All data is stored only on this computer
  - Passwords are encrypted (never stored as plain text)
  - UPS credentials are encrypted in a local file
  - Sessions automatically expire after 15 minutes of
    inactivity — you will be logged out automatically
  - Employees cannot access Settings or Logs
  - No data is ever sent anywhere except:
      UPS API (when processing shipments)
      Gmail API (when reading emails)

====================================================
  BACKUP
====================================================

To back up everything, copy the entire folder:
  C:\ShippingSystem\

to a USB drive or external hard drive.

The most important file is shipping.db —
it contains all your clients and shipment history.


====================================================
  QUICK REFERENCE — DAILY USE
====================================================

MORNING ROUTINE (Employee):
  1. Double-click start.bat
  2. Log in
  3. Click "Check Emails Now" to catch overnight emails
  4. Review Dashboard for any pending shipments

PROCESSING SHIPMENTS (Admin):
  1. Go to Shipments page
  2. Review the list carefully
  3. Click "Confirm & Process All"
  4. Labels are saved automatically to the labels folder

ADDING A CLIENT MANUALLY:
  1. Go to Clients
  2. Click "Add Client"
  3. Fill in their info and click Save
  4. Click the truck icon next to their name to queue a shipment

REVIEWING FLAGGED EMAILS:
  1. Go to Logs
  2. Search "Flagged"
  3. Read the details to see what was extracted
  4. Go to Clients → Add Client to enter them manually
