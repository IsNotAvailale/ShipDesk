Gmail Setup

To connect Gmail, place your gmail_credentials.json file (downloaded from Google Cloud Console) into:

C:\ShippingSystem\credentials\gmail_credentials.json

The first time you click Check Emails Now on the dashboard, a browser window will open asking you to authorize Gmail access. After that it runs automatically.
