====================================================
  ShipDesk Setup Guide
  Plain English — No Technical Experience Needed
====================================================

WHAT IS SHIPDESK?
ShipDesk is a program that runs on your computer and opens in your web browser.
It helps you manage client shipping information, read emails, and print UPS labels.
Nothing is stored online — everything stays on this computer.


STEP 1 — INSTALL PYTHON
------------------------------------------------------
Python is a free program that ShipDesk needs to run.

1. Open your web browser and go to: https://www.python.org/downloads/
2. Click the big yellow "Download Python" button
3. Run the installer you just downloaded
4. IMPORTANT: On the first screen, check the box that says
   "Add Python to PATH" before clicking Install Now
5. Click "Install Now" and wait for it to finish
6. Click "Close" when done


STEP 2 — SET UP GMAIL ACCESS (for reading shipping emails)
------------------------------------------------------
This lets ShipDesk read your company Gmail inbox.
You only need to do this once.

1. Go to https://console.cloud.google.com/ and sign in with
   your company Google account
2. Click "Create Project", give it any name (e.g. "ShipDesk"), click Create
3. In the search bar at the top, type "Gmail API" and press Enter
4. Click "Gmail API" in the results, then click "Enable"
5. Click "OAuth consent screen" on the left side
   - Choose "Internal" if available, or "External"
   - Fill in App Name: ShipDesk, and your email address
   - Click Save and Continue through the rest
6. Click "Credentials" on the left side
7. Click "+ Create Credentials" at the top → choose "OAuth client ID"
8. For Application type, choose "Desktop app"
9. Click Create, then click "Download JSON"
10. Rename the downloaded file to: gmail_credentials.json
11. Move it to: C:\ShippingSystem\credentials\gmail_credentials.json


STEP 3 — SET UP UPS API (for generating shipping labels)
------------------------------------------------------
1. Go to https://developer.ups.com/ and create a free account
2. Log in, go to "My Apps" and create a new app
3. Select "I want to integrate UPS technology into my business"
4. After creating the app, you will see a Client ID and Client Secret
5. Write these down — you will enter them in ShipDesk Settings later
6. Also note your UPS Account Number (6 characters, found in UPS.com account)


STEP 4 — START SHIPDESK
------------------------------------------------------
1. Open the folder: C:\ShippingSystem\
2. Double-click the file: start.bat
3. A black window will open — this is normal, do not close it
4. Your web browser will open automatically to the ShipDesk login page
5. The first time it runs, it installs some required files (takes 1-2 minutes)

Default login:
  Username: admin
  Password: admin123

IMPORTANT: Change this password right away in Settings!


STEP 5 — ENTER YOUR UPS CREDENTIALS
------------------------------------------------------
1. Log in to ShipDesk
2. Click "Settings" in the top menu
3. Under "UPS API Credentials", enter:
   - Your UPS Client ID
   - Your UPS Client Secret
   - Your UPS Account Number
   - Your company's name and address (this is the "from" address on labels)
4. Click "Save UPS Settings"


STEP 6 — CONNECT GMAIL
------------------------------------------------------
1. Make sure you completed Step 2 above
2. Click "Check Emails Now" on the Dashboard
3. A browser window will open asking you to sign in to Google
4. Sign in with your company Gmail account
5. Click "Allow" to give ShipDesk permission to read emails
6. The window will close and ShipDesk will check for new emails


YOU'RE ALL SET!
------------------------------------------------------
ShipDesk will now:
- Check your Gmail every 30 minutes automatically
- Add shipping clients it finds in emails
- Let you review and approve shipments before anything is sent to UPS

To start ShipDesk each day, just double-click: C:\ShippingSystem\start.bat
